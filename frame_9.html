Following the dissolution of the Soviet Union in 1991, which tragically also brought about the collapse of the state's pre-existing economy, Azerbaijan attained independence. Nevertheless, Azerbaijan was successful in luring several foreign oil corporations to contribute to the nation's efforts to extract and export Caspian Sea oil. When Azerbaijan eventually managed to export oil in 2005, it sparked the Second Oil Boom. Baku gained a great deal of international attention from this event, which was further increased by the city's hosting of significant international events like the yearly Formula 1 Race, the European Games, and the Eurovision Song Contest.
