Fire temple of Baku
The Ateshgah of Baku, Azerbaijani:, often called the "Fire Temple of Baku", is a castle-like religious temple in Surakhany town (in Surakhany raion),a suburb in Baku, Azerbaijan.



Based on Iranian and Indian inscriptions, the temple was used as a Hindu, Sikh, and Zoroastrian place of worship. "Ātash" (آتش) is the Persian word for fire.
