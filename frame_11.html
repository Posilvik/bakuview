Baku in 2040
The General Plan for Baku covers a 20-year perspective for further modernization and development of the city in multiple ways,
including capital construction, polycentric urban development, transport infrastructure, social infrastructure, utility systems and other measures for site development. The general plan defines measures to be implemented in all these areas of development for the capital's further modernization. Activities are planned in these areas in order to create and support sustainable development in the capital and a beneficial urban environment. They will include defining new parameters for housing, planned business zones for both public and private use, public transport, green areas, engineering opportunities and facilities.
